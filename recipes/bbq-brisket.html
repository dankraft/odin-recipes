Description


This is a classic BBQ recipe for making smoked brisket. Brikset is renowned for its savory flavor and melt in your mouth texture. It is also known for what a challening cook it can be. By following these instructions and exercising patience, you too can make a delicious brikset.

Ingredients

  • 12-18 lbs brisket
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Optional: BBQ rub of your choice like 4Rivers Coffee Rub
  • Butcher Paper

Steps

  1. Remove brisket from packagine and trim off excess fat. Try to keep fat cap layer to around 1/4" or so.
  2. Cover brisket in either salt/pepper or bbq rub of your choice.
  3. Optional: Allow brisket to sit with rub in refrigerater for several hours or overnight.
  4. Start smoker and get temperature running low between 175-225째F.
  5. Insert meat thermometer probe into brisket.
  6. Once smoker is at temp, place brisket on smoker and let it sit for about 5 hours.
  7. After 5 hours, increase smoker temp to 275째F or so.
  8. Optional: Once internal temperature of brisket gets to 165째F or so, you can wrap brisket in butcher paper and put back on the smoker
  9. Continue smoking brisket until internal temperature reaches 195-205째F.
  10. Probe should feel like it's going into butter when brikset is done. Take brisket off smoker and allow to rest wrapped and covered for one hour.
  11. After rest, slice brikset against the grain.
